# Profile Photo Instructions

## How to Add Your Photo

1. **Prepare your photo:**
   - Recommended size: 500x500px or larger
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
   - Professional headshot or portrait

2. **Add your photo:**
   - Save your photo as `profile.jpg` or `profile.png` in this folder
   - If using PNG, update `index.html` line with the photo to use `.png` extension

3. **Update the HTML:**
   - Open `index.html`
   - Find: `<img src="images/profile-placeholder.svg"`
   - Replace with: `<img src="images/profile.jpg"` (or `.png`)

## Current Placeholder

The placeholder SVG (`profile-placeholder.svg`) will display until you add your actual photo.

## Tips for Best Results

- Use good lighting
- Plain or blurred background works best
- Professional attire recommended
- Eye contact with camera
- Natural expression
- High resolution (at least 500x500px)
